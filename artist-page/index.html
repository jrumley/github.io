Establishing a web page for your music project like a band or solo venture shouldn’t be a task that takes away from your production time. But you know that it’s essential to have a home for your web presence online.

Maybe you’re using a template-based service but you’re already seeing the limitations. Your site looks like every other site out there and there’s no support for the kind of ideas you’re thinking of.
Let me help you.

I’ve been in this exact spot with my own projects. Frustrated by the limitations imposed by template sites, I learned the tools and fundamentals of web design. But I also learned a little about music marketing.

Musicians are unique in that they need more than just a place for fans to come to. They also need an interface to their business contacts such as promotes and event coordinators. Commonly known as an Electronic Press Kit (or EPK), this will highlight your accomplishments and make you more attractive to do business with.
